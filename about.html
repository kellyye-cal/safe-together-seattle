Safe Together Seattle

We are a group of developers passionate about using design and technology to improve the safety of our communities.

Safe Together Seattle was created to ensure vulnerable communities felt safe walking home alone at night. As women living in big cities ourselves, we understand the concern and fear of walking anywhere alone in the dark. We want people to know like they are not alone and can use Safe Together Seattle as a resource to keep them safe.
